# A Sample Blog Article

This sample post exists to prove the shared-root layout. The article route loads MDX from `src/content/blog`, the blog index reads article metadata from the page map, and the blog route files pull in images that still live beside the article content.

## Why this structure works

- Each article lives inside its own folder.
- The item route resolves `index.mdx` at `/blog/some-article`.
- The root blog page can read article copy from the normalized Nextra page map.
- The blog route files can still import hero and preview imagery from the article folder.

## What to customize next

Swap the solid SVGs for real editorial artwork, replace this copy with launch notes or product essays, and continue adding article folders under `src/content/blog` as the blog grows.
